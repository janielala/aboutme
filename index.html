About me

Hi there! I'm Janie, a designer who finds joy in blending aesthetics with coding. I've always had a fascination with visuals and their ability to convey emotions and messages. As I started my career in design, I discovered the potential of coding to bring my creative ideas to life. I delved into coding languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, exploring how they could enhance my designs with interactivity and a seamless user experience.

With a balanced mix of design skills and coding knowledge, I approach projects from a well-rounded perspective. I strive to create visually appealing designs while also ensuring they perform well, are accessible, and work smoothly across various devices and platforms. My goal is to craft digital experiences that leave a positive and lasting impact.

I'm always on the lookout for new opportunities to learn and grow. Attending workshops and collaborating with fellow creatives keeps me updated on the latest design trends and coding techniques. I believe in the power of design and technology to solve problems and foster meaningful connections. With a continuous thirst for knowledge and a passion for pushing the boundaries of design and coding, I'm excited to contribute to the world of digital experiences in my own humble way.
